EMERGING TECHNOLOGY

NEW TECH COMING OUTTA THE CRACKS

About

Over the past few years as technology advance, new technology which is unfamiliar emerges and begins to be used by itself or implemented with technology we use today.           

A concept like this explores how technology is changing now and is going to change in the future to tend to specifications of a client and complete tasks in a more effective manner than current devices or technology.       

Traditionally, these development trends follow specific specification based areas such as CPU power and output, device size, memory and screen resolution but, as of late, an array of interesting trends have emerged whcih stray from traditional lines such as biometric security, voice acivation/voice control and the increasing number of cameras.         

PRO

CAN LEAD TO FURTHER INNOVATION IN THE TECH SPHERE 

Emerging technology can allow for further development in technology as it can reveal new ideas and provide solutions for current problems  

PRO

CAN IMPROVE PEOPLE'S EXPERIENCE WITH TECHNOLOGY

Emerging technology can make peoples experience with technology better. Wether it is more efficient, more safe etc.    

CON

EMERGING TECHNOLOGY IS NOT REFINED 

Emerging technology is not as polished as technology that is currently in circulation. It can take years for emerging technology to be fully polishes       

CON

EMERGING TECHNOLOGY CAN BE PRICEY

Innovation involves reasearch and development which can cost a lot of money for the developer to go through that  procces.
