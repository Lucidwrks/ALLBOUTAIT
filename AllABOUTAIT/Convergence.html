About

Technology convergence is the process by which existing technology merges into new forms that bring together different types of media and applications

Through tech convergence, devices have the possibility to interact with a wider array of media types.

As the technology advances, new models might include additional features such as the ability to interface with more devices and play other types of media.

PRO 

THE CONSUMER HAS MULTIPLE DEVICES IN ONE DEVICE 

This makes owning one devices with multiple technologies more conveniet and much easier to access for the consumer    

PRO

SAVES THE CONSUMER MONEY

Money that would'e gone to buying multiple specialised devices can now be invested in other things the consumer needs due to convergence        

CON

INITIAL TRADE OFF IN QUALITY

Converged devices will have a trade off in their quality as they are not specialisd to complete 1 task      

CON

DEDICATED DEVICES BECOME OBSELETE

Although there is a trade off in quality, it is only initial and converged devices can match and even exceed the quality of specialised devices

EXAMPLE  1: THE SMARTPHONE

Mobile phone with an advanced operating system combining features of a pc operating system with other useful features intended for hand held use. Smart phones typically combine the features of a cell phone with those of other popular mobile devices, such as personal digital assistant (PDA), media player and GPS navigation unit. Most smartphones can access the Internet, have a touchscreen user interface, with either an LCD, OLED, AMOLED, LED or similar screen, can run third-party apps, music players and are camera phones.

EXAMPLE 2: THE VIDEO GAME CONSOLE

modern video game developers may create consoles primarily for playing games, but they also design them to play back video and music and to connect to the Internet. Similarly, new media players are capable of not only playing video or audio from a physical medium, but can also stream data over the Internet, display photographs on a disc, and view websites online. Where multiple pieces of home entertainment equipment were once necessary, a single device may provide all of the functionality required.
